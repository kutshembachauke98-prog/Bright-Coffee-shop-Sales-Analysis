## Bright Coffee Shop Sales Analysis

1. ## Problem Description
Bright Coffee Shop recently appointed a new CEO focused on growing company revenue and improving product performance. The business lacked a deep understanding of its daily transactional data, specifically regarding product-level performance and temporal sales trends. As a Junior Data Analyst, the challenge was to transform raw historical data into actionable insights to assist the CEO in strategic decision-making.  

2. ## Aim of the Project
The primary aim of this project was to analyze historical sales records to identify key revenue drivers, peak operational times, and customer behavior patterns to provide data-driven recommendations for business growth.

3. ## Objectives
- Data Flow Architecture: Design a diagram to outline the ETL pipeline from source to presentation.
- Transformation: Process raw data by converting formats and creating time-based buckets for analysis.
- Revenue Identification: Determine which products and categories generate the most revenue.
- Trend Analysis: Analyze sales patterns across different times of the day, days of the week, and months.
-  Actionable Reporting: Provide recommendations for inventory, staffing, and marketing.

 4. ## Tools Used
- Databricks SQL: For data cleaning, transformations, and querying.
- Microsoft Excel: For generating PivotTables, charts, and the final dashboard.
- GitHub: For version control and storing SQL scripts.
- Canva: For presenting insights and recommendations to the CEO.

5. ## Summary of Results
- Top Performer: The Hell's Kitchen location generated the highest revenue, likely due to high foot traffic.
- Key Products: While Coffee is the most profitable category , the Chocolate Croissant is the top-selling individual item with approximately 3,300 units sold.
- Peak Times: 72% of revenue is generated on weekdays , with the morning hours serving as the busiest period for all locations.
- Growth Trend: Revenue showed a steady increase from March through June, with June being the peak month.

7. ## Recommendations
- Staffing: Increase staff levels at the Hell's Kitchen location during morning peaks to maintain service quality.
- Inventory: Prioritize coffee bean stock and ensure high-volume bakery items like Chocolate Croissants are always available.
- Promotions: Launch targeted marketing campaigns or "combo deals" during slower afternoon and weekend periods to balance revenue.
