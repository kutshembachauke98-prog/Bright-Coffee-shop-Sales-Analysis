# Bright-Coffee-shop-Sales-Analysis
**Objective**
This project conducts a comprehensive sales analysis of Bright Coffee Shop over the last 6 months. 
The purpose of this analysis is to provide insights into the shop's performance, including revenue trends, 
top-selling products, peak sales hours, and customer behavior patterns. 
The findings will help the CEO and management team make informed decisions to optimize sales, 
product offerings, and operational efficiency.

**Tools Used**
Databricks SQL
Microsoft Excel / picot tables & charts
Canva

##3.Methods
Sales data for Bright Coffee Shop over the last six months was first **collected from Bright Coffee Shop's point--of-sale system** then **prepared  and cleaned in Databricks**.
The structured data was then analyzed in **Excel** using**Pivot tables and Charts** to summarize revenue by month ' store location, time of day, and top-selling products. Final visualizations and reports were designed in Excel and Canva.

**Key Insights**
-**Hell's Kitchen** location generated the highest revenue, with June being the peak month(likely due to seasonal demand).
-Chocolate croissant was the top-selling product, driving most bakery revenue.
-Coffee was the most profitable category, followed by tea and bakery items
-Morning hours had the highest sales, gradually decreasing throughout the day.
- Weekdays accounted for 72% of the total revenue, showing daily routines drive most purchases.
